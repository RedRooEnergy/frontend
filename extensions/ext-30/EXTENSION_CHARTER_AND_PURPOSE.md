# EXT-30 — Extension Charter & Purpose

Status: GOVERNANCE DRAFT  
Extension: EXT-30 — Marketing Intelligence, Creative & Campaign Studio  
Implementation: NOT AUTHORIZED

## Purpose

EXT-30 establishes a governed, auditable system for researching,
ideating, drafting, and preparing marketing campaigns and creative
assets in support of the RedRooEnergy marketplace.

EXT-30 is a decision-support and creative-production extension only.
It must not autonomously publish, distribute, or execute marketing.

## Extension Charter

EXT-30 exists to:
- Monitor approved sources for marketing and promotion insights
- Translate insights into structured marketing ideas and campaigns
- Produce approval-ready creative assets
- Enforce brand, compliance, evidence, and approval governance

EXT-30 is subordinate to Core authority at all times.

## Authoritative Principles

- Governance-first: no bypass of review or approval
- Human-in-the-loop: all outbound activity requires approval
- Evidence-bound claims: no unsupported marketing claims
- Full auditability: all artefacts versioned and attributable

## Intended Users

- Marketplace Administrator (Marketing Authority)
- Compliance Authority
- Marketing Editors
- System (draft generation only)

## Explicit Non-Objectives

EXT-30 MUST NOT:
- Autonomously send emails or publish content
- Bypass legal, brand, or compliance doctrine
- Scrape unlicensed content
- Generate or use imagery without rights metadata

## Governance Dependency

EXT-30 is governed by:
- The Immutable Core
- Extensions Governance Framework
- Core audit, privacy, and compliance doctrine

## Non-Operational Status

EXT-30 is governance-only at this stage.
No operational activity is authorised.

## Audit Expectation

All future EXT-30 actions MUST be auditable.

## Out of Scope

- Technical architecture
- Automation logic
- Distribution infrastructure
