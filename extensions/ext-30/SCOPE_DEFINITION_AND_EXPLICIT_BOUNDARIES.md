# EXT-30 — Scope Definition & Explicit Boundaries

Status: GOVERNANCE DRAFT  
Extension: EXT-30 — Marketing Intelligence, Creative & Campaign Studio  
Implementation: NOT AUTHORIZED

## Purpose

Defines scope boundaries governing EXT-30.

## In-Scope Capabilities

- Marketing research from approved sources
- Idea generation and campaign drafting
- Creative asset preparation (copy, layout, imagery)
- Approval workflows and intent records
- Post-campaign learning ingestion

## Explicit Exclusions

EXT-30 MUST NOT:
- Send or publish content
- Acquire contact lists
- Override compliance or Core rules
- Use unlicensed assets

## Hard Governance Gates

No asset may reach Approved without:
- Brand compliance
- Claims evidence
- Image rights metadata
- Approval signatures
- Hash-locked artefacts

## Failure on Boundary Ambiguity

If scope is unclear, action MUST be denied.

## Audit Requirements

All scope denials MUST be auditable.

## Out of Scope

- Automation engines
- Messaging infrastructure


Validation Checklist:

Scope defined  
Exclusions enforced  
Governance gates stated  

STOP POINT
