# EXT-30 — Data Ownership & Evidence Boundaries

Status: GOVERNANCE DRAFT  
Extension: EXT-30 — Marketing Intelligence, Creative & Campaign Studio  
Implementation: NOT AUTHORIZED

## Purpose

Defines data and evidence boundaries.

## Data Ownership

EXT-30 owns:
- Draft creative assets
- Research notes
- Campaign metadata

EXT-30 does not own Core data.

## Evidence Rules

Claims require linked evidence.
Evidence cannot be altered.

## Failure Behaviour

Unclear ownership results in denial.

## Audit Requirements

All data actions MUST be auditable.

## Out of Scope

- Backup systems
- Export tooling


Validation Checklist:

Ownership defined  
Evidence enforced  

STOP POINT
