This document defines audit events for the records and evidence lifecycle.

Events include upload, access, linking, export, retention, and legal hold.

Examples (non-exhaustive):
- RECORD_UPLOAD_ATTEMPTED
- RECORD_CREATED
- RECORD_LINKED
- RECORD_ACCESSED
- RECORD_EXPORTED
- RECORD_MUTATION_ATTEMPTED
- RECORD_DELETION_ATTEMPTED
- EVIDENCE_PACK_GENERATED

All events are auditable and immutable.
