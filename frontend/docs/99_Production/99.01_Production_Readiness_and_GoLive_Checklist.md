## Secrets & Key Management
- Use env vars; no secrets committed.
- Separate keys for dev/staging/prod; rotate before launch.

## Webhook Security & Rate Limiting
- Verify signatures (Stripe, Wise); enforce idempotency.
- Apply IP allowlists/rate limits where supported.

## Environment Separation
- Distinct endpoints, keys, and data stores for dev/stage/prod.
- Disable test headers and sandbox shortcuts in production.

## Rollback & Incident Response
- Playbook for payment failures, webhook outages, settlement errors.
- Rollback plan with clear triggers and responsible roles.

## Launch Sign-Off Authority
- Final go/no-go requires approvals: Product, Engineering, Compliance/Legal.
- Record sign-off IDs and timestamps.
