# Supplier Onboarding UX Governance
Version: v1.0 (LOCKED)  
Scope: Governance-only; no implementation

## Required Onboarding Steps
- KYB capture and verification.
- Compliance declarations (product categories, export controls).
- Payment capability declaration (accepted rails, currencies).
- Acceptance of full-prepayment default.

## Payment Capability Declaration
- Supplier must state: supported payout currencies, preferred rail (Wise/Airwallex/SWIFT), account beneficiary alignment to KYB entity.
- Full-payment flag stored at onboarding; staging allowed only after waiver.

## Supplier Acknowledgement
- Explicit acknowledgement of payout rules: escrow hold, settlement eligibility, return-window dependency.
- Agreement that off-platform payments are prohibited.
