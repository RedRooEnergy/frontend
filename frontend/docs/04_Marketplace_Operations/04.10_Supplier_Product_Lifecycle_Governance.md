# Supplier Product Lifecycle Governance
Version: v1.0 (LOCKED)  
Scope: Governance-only; no implementation

## Lifecycle Stages
- Submission → Review → Approval → Listing → Sale → Fulfilment → Delivery → Return Window → Settlement.

## Payment Dependencies
- Pricing snapshot required at sale.
- Escrow hold initiated on payment success.
- Settlement eligibility only after delivery proof and return-window expiry (unless admin override).

## Hold/Release Conditions
- Hold if compliance docs missing, shipment evidence absent, or disputes active.
- Release when eligibility met and no active disputes/returns/warranties impacting payout.

## Evidence Expectations
- Product approvals must capture certifications per category.
- Shipment stage must capture carrier, tracking, and timestamped proof.
