## Page Type Catalogue

### 1. Public Narrative Pages
Examples:
- Homepage
- About
- How It Works
- Compliance & Trust
- Contact

Structure:
- Linear content flow
- Section-based layout
- Trust-first information hierarchy

Role:
- Establish credibility
- Explain governance and enforcement
- Guide users to role-specific entry points

---

### 2. Marketplace Browsing Pages
Examples:
- Marketplace landing
- Category pages
- Sub-category pages

Structure:
- Hierarchical navigation
- Persistent left-hand category tree
- Modular content sections

Role:
- Enable discovery
- Present products within governed categories
- Surface trust and compliance signals early

---

### 3. Product Detail Pages
Examples:
- Product view

Structure:
- Primary product information
- Compliance and certification visibility
- Pricing snapshot indicators (read-only)

Role:
- Inform purchase decisions
- Reinforce enforcement (locked pricing, verified compliance)
- Prevent ambiguity

---

### 4. Transactional Pages
Examples:
- Cart
- Checkout
- Order confirmation

Structure:
- State-driven layout
- Minimal distraction
- Clear progress indicators

Role:
- Execute governed transactions
- Reflect enforced pricing and permissions
- Prevent user error

---

### 5. Buyer Dashboard Pages
Examples:
- Buyer dashboard
- Buyer orders
- Buyer order detail
- Buyer documents

Structure:
- Summary-first
- Drill-down capability
- Read-only enforcement reflection

Role:
- Provide clarity on order state
- Surface documents and pricing snapshots
- Communicate enforced outcomes

---

### 6. Supplier Dashboard Pages
Examples:
- Supplier dashboard
- Supplier products
- Supplier orders
- Supplier compliance

Structure:
- Status-driven panels
- Clear enforcement indicators
- Action availability explained

Role:
- Enable supplier participation
- Reflect compliance and enforcement truth
- Prevent scope overreach

---

### 7. Admin & Governance Pages
Examples:
- Admin dashboard
- Audit views
- Extension visibility
- Compliance oversight

Structure:
- Data-dense but read-only
- Filters and summaries
- Escalation pathways (not actions)

Role:
- Provide oversight
- Support audit and governance
- Avoid override affordances

---

## Non-Negotiables

- Every page must conform to one page type
- Page types define structure, not styling
- No hybrid or ad-hoc page types
- Enforcement outcomes must be visible where relevant
