# Homepage Wireframe — Phase E

Version: v1.0 DRAFT
Governance State: CORE + EXTENSIONS LOCKED
Design Doctrine: LOCKED
Enforcement State: LIVE

Purpose:
Define the homepage as a sequence of modular sections that establish
trust, explain governance, and enable marketplace discovery.

---

## Section Order & Purpose

### Section 1 — Trust & Authority Header
Purpose:
- Immediately establish platform credibility
- Signal governed, enforced marketplace

Content:
- Platform statement (what this is)
- Governance & enforcement assurance
- Primary role-based entry points (Buyer / Supplier)

Rule:
- No pricing or promotions here
- Authority before commerce

---

### Section 2 — How the Marketplace Works
Purpose:
- Explain process, not features
- Reduce cognitive load for first-time users

Content:
- Simple step sequence (Browse → Lock Price → Verified Compliance → Delivery)
- Emphasis on enforcement and auditability

Rule:
- Plain language
- No technical jargon

---

### Section 3 — Shop Now (Marketplace Entry)
Purpose:
- Provide clear entry to governed marketplace

Content:
- Top-level categories
- Governed category grouping

Rule:
- No personalised recommendations at this stage
- Discovery over persuasion

---

### Section 4 — Recommended for You
Purpose:
- Surface relevant products without manipulation

Content:
- Algorithmic or rules-based recommendations
- Clear labelling of recommendation logic

Rule:
- No dark patterns
- Must not imply flexible pricing

---

### Section 5 — What’s Getting the Most Attention
Purpose:
- Social proof through activity, not hype

Content:
- High-interaction products
- Popular categories

Rule:
- Calm presentation
- No urgency language

---

### Section 6 — Trending
Purpose:
- Show market momentum

Content:
- Trending products or categories
- Time-bounded relevance

Rule:
- Informational, not promotional

---

### Section 7 — Today’s Best Deals
Purpose:
- Commercial competitiveness within governance

Content:
- Governed discounts
- Locked pricing indicators

Rule:
- Must respect pricing snapshot enforcement
- No flash-sale behaviour

---

### Section 8 — Shopping Tools & Service Partners
Purpose:
- Present third-party services as first-class participants

Content:
- Freight & logistics
- Compliance & certification
- Warranty & service
- Installers & electricians

Rule:
- Structured, professional presentation
- No widget-like treatment

---

### Section 9 — Governance & Compliance Assurance
Purpose:
- Reinforce trust before conversion

Content:
- Audit immutability
- Compliance verification
- Enforcement guarantees

Rule:
- Factual, verifiable statements only

---

### Section 10 — Footer Trust Closure
Purpose:
- Provide reassurance and closure

Content:
- Governance links
- Compliance references
- Legal & audit information

Rule:
- No calls to action that bypass governance

---

## Non-Negotiables

- Homepage is modular and reorderable only via governance
- Each section has a single purpose
- No section implies flexibility where enforcement exists
- Trust precedes conversion throughout
